Gridlex

Just a Flexbox Grid System

Simple. Robust. Flexible.

How it works?
The concept is simple: you need to wrap your .col in a .grid.
That's all.

Gridlex and media-queries

Because of responsive, you need sometimes to change the size of columns: with this keys as classNames you can control your layout by media-queries.

Key CSS Media Query Applies Classname
- Always .col-
xs @media screen and (max-width: 35.5em) up to 568px .col-xs-*
sm- @media screen and (min-width: 35.5em) and (max-width: 48em) from 568px to 768px .col-sm-*
md @media screen and (min-width: 48em) and (max-width: 64em) from 768px to 1024px .col-md-*
lg @media screen and (min-width: 64em) and (max-width: 80em) from 1024px to 1280px .col-lg-*
xl @media screen and (min-width: 80em) from 1280px .col-xl-*
